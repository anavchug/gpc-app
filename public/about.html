About Us

Welcome to GalaxyGameSavers, your source for the latest and greatest deals on video games! Whether you're a casual gamer or a hardcore enthusiast, we've got you covered.

Mission

Our goal with our project is to make finding deals for games that much easier. With so many game sellers, it can be hard to keep track of when discounts on games come, and by the time you realize it, it might be too late! Our mission is to provide you with a one-stop shop to check out game deals from various sellers, such as Steam, Humble Bundle, Gog, and many more.
